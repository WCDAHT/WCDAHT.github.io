About BitEU

BitEU is dedicated to creating professional-grade digital forensics tools that are freely available to investigators, researchers, and security professionals worldwide. Our open-source approach ensures transparency, community-driven development, and continuous improvement.

Each tool in our suite addresses specific forensic challenges, from vehicle telematics analysis to memory forensics, providing investigators with the specialized capabilities they need to uncover digital evidence effectively.
